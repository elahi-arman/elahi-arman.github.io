PURCHASING TICKETS


Fill out the form to your right just so we can have your tickets on record!



Purchasing tickets this year is a little bit different. In order to have everything logged, all transactions will be logged not only with your physical ticket, but also through a Google form and in our records. For payment, you or your liason can pay either Arman Elahi or Pranav Bheda in cash or through Venmo and we'll have your ticket delivered to you.


If you choose to Venmo, please tag the payment with "Culture Show, X Tickets". Where X is the number of tickets you purchase. If the correct amount is not paid, we will Venmo you back your money.
